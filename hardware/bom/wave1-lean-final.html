Sensor Signal Chain 4 channels

INA128 amplifies the sEMG signal at the electrode. ESP32's 12-bit ADC digitizes it — garbage for spectral analysis, but enough to see "muscle on/off" and validate placements. Wave 2 adds the ADS1292R or Cerelog for real resolution.
