On the Body
Erector spinae (lower back) + Quadriceps (front thigh)

● Erector Spinae — Deadlift & Squat Back Safety

Detects fatigue in the lower back muscles before form breaks down. EMG median frequency shifts downward as the erector spinae fatigues — this happens 2–5 reps BEFORE your back visibly rounds. The camera sees the rounding; the EMG predicts it.

Median frequency decline is the accepted biomarker for localized muscle fatigue (Jamaluddin et al., 2023). RMS amplitude increases as additional motor units are recruited to compensate for fatiguing fibers (Frontiers in Rehab Sciences, 2022).

● Quadriceps (VL vs VM) — Knee Valgus Detection

When the vastus medialis (inner quad) fatigues before the vastus lateralis (outer quad), the knee collapses inward — the #1 mechanism for ACL injury in squats. The camera sees the knee move; the EMG tells you WHY.

H:Q activation imbalance is a primary contributing factor to ACL injury; women exhibit reduced ratios indicating quad dominance (Cal State thesis). Malaligned squats show decreased VL/VM/RF activation with increased biceps femoris and gastrocnemius compensation (J Strength Cond Res, 2017).

● Real-Time Cueing — "Activate This Muscle"

When camera detects knee valgus or EMG detects compensation (erector spinae overloading instead of glutes), a vibration at the correct location tells the beginner WHERE to focus. Replicates the trainer's tap — "squeeze here."

Vibrotactile localization accuracy is 60.7% on the torso (Deshpande 2021). Vibration excels at "where" — grabbing attention and pointing to a location. Your friend's six-dimension analysis: vibration covers 2/6 cue dimensions well (location + timing).

● Pre-Rep Warning — "Your Back Is Fatiguing"

Between reps, a distinct vibration pattern (double-pulse vs single) signals EMG fatigue markers have crossed threshold. Beginner learns: this pattern = reduce weight or stop.

Pre-rep cueing is more effective than mid-rep correction for motor learning (Muscle Minder finding, friend's research). Vibration onset is 5–100ms — fast enough for between-rep alerts. Budget coin ERMs sufficient for binary on/off at MVP.

● Wiring the Closed Loop

Connects MyoWare analog out → ESP32 ADC. ESP32 GPIO → MOSFET gate → vibration motor. Breadboard lets you iterate before soldering. 2N7002 MOSFETs switch motors from 3.3V logic — one per motor.
